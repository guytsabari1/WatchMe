About Me

My name is Guy Tsabari, I am 21 years old.
In the past 3 years, I served as a combat warrior, commander and sergeant in the Israel Defense Forces.
My specialty was rescuing people who were trapped under ruined buildings during global natural disasters.
During my service, I served as commander in the unit's command course and a sergeant in the basic training course.
All my life I've loved walking around with a watch on my hand, I think watches are a real work of art and they can make any simple person look like someone important, Now it's your turn to look for the right watch for you!
